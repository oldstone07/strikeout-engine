★ Forward test — the real one

One bet, three candidates — all logged live at real prices. Each is graded on ROI (price-aware, so juiced and cheap tracks compare fairly), bar ROI ≥ +5%. Value (Strong + ≤5.5) is the only thing you bet, over 30 picks. Conviction Over is a candidate over 50. Overpower (Over + line ≥5.5) is a candidate over 40. Nothing earns money until its track clears +5% ROI. Only live picks count here.

Weekly hit rate

Each point is one week's hit rate for the window. The flat reference line is break-even (52.4% at -110). Steadier line = more consistent edge; a saw-tooth that dips below break-even is a signal leaning on hot streaks.

What counts as a bet?

A pick is only called BET on the Today tab once its type of disagreement has cleared a statistical bar in your own graded history (≥30 samples, statistically beats break-even). Until then it's SIT OUT — priced but unproven. That silence is the tool being honest.
